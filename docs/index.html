FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about BrawlIQ.

Is BrawlIQ really free?
Yes, completely free. No premium tier, no hidden fees. You can look up any player tag without even creating an account.
Where does the data come from?
All data comes directly from the official Brawl Stars API by Supercell. BrawlIQ doesn't estimate or guess anything — every number reflects real battle data.
How much battle history will I have?
The Brawl Stars API returns your last 25 battles per fetch. BrawlIQ stores every batch it retrieves, so the longer you're tracked, the more history you accumulate. Check in regularly to avoid gaps in your data.
Do I need to log in?
No — you can look up any player tag without creating an account. Sign in with Google to save up to 4 tags and get automatic background refresh every 30 minutes.
What is a Player Tag?
Your unique Brawl Stars identifier, like #ABC123. Find it in-game by tapping your profile picture at the top left of the home screen.
What is the Community Meta?
Aggregated win rate, pick rate, and star rate across all BrawlIQ players. Each player tag is counted only once to prevent duplicate data. The more players use BrawlIQ, the more accurate and representative the meta becomes.
What is tilt detection?
BrawlIQ groups your battles into sessions (battles within 30 minutes of each other) and flags any session that had a losing streak of 4 or more. It's a reminder to step away when things are going badly rather than digging a deeper hole.
Is BrawlIQ affiliated with Supercell?
No. BrawlIQ is a fan-made tool that uses the public Brawl Stars API. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Supercell in any way.
